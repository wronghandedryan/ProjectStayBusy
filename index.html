Keep Your Mind Busy


There are plenty of reasons to stay busy, boredom, success, the list could go on and on. However, that is not why we created this site. We are focused on keeping your brain stimulated to maintain mental health while the Covid-19 global pandemic is happening and social distancing is being implemented. Our site is designed to keep you busy while also being a haven from the overwhelming news that bleeds into every industry. If you visit any website, there is almost always immediately a section addressing the issue. You cannot look at any major news site without the crisis effecting every category. This can be very daunting since it feels like our lives are now becoming defined by this pandemic. This site is no different except this page is the only part of our page that directly addresses it. Throughout the rest of the site you will not find any mention of the virus, but ways to stay busy and not be consumed by the news/or talk of the crisis. Almost any other site will provide you with major news about the pandemic, but here is a space where you can escape; you can just focus on staying stimulated without being consumed by the overwhelming magnitude of this global event. We hope you find this useful, but at the very least us as developers used this for the same reason, intentional or not. Lets keep that beautiful brain of yours busy. We hope you all stay safe, healthy, and especially sane!
